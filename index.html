Website and Branding design

Ernest Mercier Lecture


Website and Branding design

The Ernest Mercier Lecture series is an annual conference dedicated to bridging the worlds of business and commerce with the intensive research world of chemistry.

CUSEC


Branding for a conference

Canadian University Software Engineering Conference is a Canadian software engineering conference based out of Montreal, Quebec. My team was in charge of the look and feel of this year's iteration of CUSEC. We decided to choose colors and a feel that stems from the engineering culture. Purple, creative thought, and strong shapes are all elements that represent engineers as a whole.
